About Us

Crafting quality ironwork inspired by resonance, beauty, and durability.

At Frequency-528 Iron Works, we blend traditional blacksmithing techniques with modern design sensibility. Every piece we create — from garden gates to custom railings — is forged with care and built to last generations.

The number 528 represents the resonant frequency associated with harmony and transformation. It's our guiding philosophy: craftsmanship that resonates with you and your space.

Whether you need a one-of-a-kind decorative piece, structural ironwork, or digital design resources to plan your next project, we're here to help.
